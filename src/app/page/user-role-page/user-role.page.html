Roles Configure groups for 'view', 'edit' and 'partner' roles View Configure groups with 'view' role. Groups with this roles are allowed 'view' access to the system. Edit Configure groups with 'edit' role. Groups with this role are allowed 'edit' access to the system. Partner Configure groups with 'partner' role. Groups with this role has no 'view' and 'edit' access to the system, but will have access to partner page where they can view items and their pricing depending on which pricing structures the group has. Pricing structures and items price are configurable in Pricing side menu.
